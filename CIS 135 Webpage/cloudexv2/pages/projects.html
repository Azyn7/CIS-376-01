I’ve worked on various projects in my time on the internet. From mod development to video production, it’s a rather robust tale of a child dreaming of becoming a game programmer. The biggest project I’ve worked on is the Left 4 Dead 2 E3 Restoration Project (officially known as the E3 Beta Pack).

[Left 4 Dead 2 E3 Group Steam Page]

While my time with the project has been short (at least compared to how long it’s been out and in development), it was easily one of the most influential experiences I’ve had so far. I helped find and edit scripts, make maps, edit textures, and so much more. You can check some of my additions by going to my steam workshop page. It has my major contributions for download seperate from the pack.

[Steam Workshop Content]

Garry’s Mod was one of my first PC games, and easily one of the most influential games in terms of making me want to develop them myself. Garry’s Mod feels less like a game and more so a game engine in a game engine. It has hundreds of gamemodes and thousands of individual and unique communities. If you haven’t played it, you definitely should! It's fairly easy to mod, and very accessible!
