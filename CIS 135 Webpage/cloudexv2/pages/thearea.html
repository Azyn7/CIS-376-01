The Area

Florence Alabama is a rather diverse place despite being in the far-left corner of North Alabama. It's home to the University of North Alabama, a variety of shops (both locally owned and corporately owned), and a setting of very artistic architecture. Driving through down-town Florence, in some places at least, is like going back in time. A lot of the areas are well-kept and look like they did when the houses were built.

University of North Alabama

UNA has an extremely gorgeous campus, which hosts a wide variety of student gathering sites. From the various classrooms to the noteworthy amount of RSOs, there is plenty to do!

[Downtown Florence Historical District]

Downtown Florence

AS mentioned, Downtown Florence is a rather diverse area of old and new! Some of it is run down; however, the rest is fairly well upkept. Closer to the University, the nicer! It's worth a visit if you are in the area.
