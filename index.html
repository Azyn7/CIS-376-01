HUMBLE BEGINNINGS

The majority of my youth, I had been highly fascinated with video games and their creation. Even now, going on twenty-one, I'm still yearning to make the same magic I had experienced growing up. I started learning gLua, which is a version of Lua made specifically for a game called Garry's Mod. My "mods", if you can even call them that, were very rudimentary. In essence, I was closer to a "script kiddie" than a "programmer"; however, a start is a start. In the words of Sir Francis Drake:
"Sic Parvis Magna" ("Greatness From Small Beginnings")
