HUMBLE BEGINNINGS

The majority of my youth, I had been highly fascinated with video games and their creation. Even now, going on twenty-one, I'm still yearning to make the same magic I had experienced growing up. I started learning gLua, which is a version of Lua made specifically for a game called Garry's Mod. My "mods", if you can even call them that, were very rudimentary. In essence, I was closer to a "script kiddie" than a "programmer"; however, a start is a start. In the words of Sir Francis Drake:
"Sic Parvis Magna" ("Greatness From Small Beginnings")

LEFT FOR DEV

I believe the year was 2021: sometime then, I was playing one of my old favorite games, Left 4 Dead 2, when I had a chance encounter with a user by the name of Gigan. Him, and the people with him, fundamentally changed my life. That group of people, which would eventually become like a family to me, was the L4D2 Beta Pack team, now branded as Sourcerer. I would go on to become the Assistant Lead for the project, second in command to Gigan, otherwise known as Peter; however, after restructuring, and a swap in roles, I became the lead Graphic Designer. The project has now essentially reached completion, and its project statement met. I did attribute a handful of script mods to the pack; however, the majority of my work was graphic work, hence the new role.

CONTENT CREATOR

I've been making content on YouTube since 2012, when I was only 9 years old. Should I have been? Probably not; however, didn't change the fact that I did. I have a passion for content creation, from scripting and editing to voice over and graphic work. I've been putting off making videos for the last few months, but I do have plenty of ideas for new videos. I typically just make what I want, when I want, how I want, with no real rhyme or reason to it, so long as I enjoy doing it. I've had several YouTube accounts over the years, with my most recent one being Wickynii.
